Where do your donations go?

As a non-profit organization, our mission remains dedicated to providing free service to all in our community. However, we often need funds for things such as rackets, balls, and hosting events such as ice cream days or tournaments! We guarantee that all of your donations are used directly on camp-related purchases. This way, you can have a direct impact on our community and join our cause! We greatly appreciate all of the support!
